Dance for Planned Parenthood

About us | Program | Feminism Club


Mission

With this event I would like to shed light on the fact that Planned Parenthood provides many women’s health services and that abortions make up only 3% of their services. And yet, this vital resource may well be defunded. Planned Parenthood provides cancer exams, contraception such as the birth control pill; it tests for and treats STIs, and provides sex education to over 1.5 million people per year. I want to make an expressive outlet for emotions on this topic into a dance show created and performed by Bentley students. This is a cause that many are passionate about, and a benefit show is a great way to get people involved who don’t know how to support or contribute otherwise. The dance show will be a repertory show choreographed and performed by Bentley students.
